Beyond making websites, I love to create using all forms of media. I'm intrigued by the art of storytelling and bringing new ideas into the world.

PROJECT 2X1 | Documentary

I was one of the founding members that created the first ever documentary using Google Glass. Our film explores the relationship between the Hasidic and West Indian communities in Crown Heights, Brooklyn. For four months we explored the community, learning people’s stories and having them film their lives through Google Glass. This was an experiment in documentation and community building. The documentary premiered to hundreds of community members and is currently being used as a catalyst for discussion between the two communities.

Photographer & Interactive Lead • 2013-2014 • Visit Project 2x1

KUUMBA TV | Creative Network & Video Platform

I founded a creative network on my college campus to document creative students and projects through video stories and profiles. Kuumba began out of a passion for bringing innovative individuals together and strengthening their presence on campus. It evolved beyond video profiles to include a speaking series and curated events across campus. Kuumba is still running and leading the charge for creativity at Washington University in St.Louis.

Founder & Creative Director • 2010-2012 • Visit Kuumba TV

Companhia Bate Palmas | Photodocumentary

I traveled for three months with a youth drum band in Brazil to document their presence and effect on the surrounding community. Through the documentary, I realized that music can have a huge impact on youth and the communities in which they live.
